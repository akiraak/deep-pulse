Discovery luxury Japanese confections from Hokkaido, Japan.  Royce' is famous for their "nama chocolate" -- a rich, chocolate ganache made of select chocolates and fresh cream.

Our steam table boasts a broad selection of new and classic Asian dishes such as orange chicken, sweet & sour pork, Mongolian beef alongside BBQ roast duck.  Our poke bar features a bright and colorful selection made fresh daily usinly only the finest ingredients.

Enjoy authentic Hawaiian comfort foods in the Pacific Northwest.  Aloha Plates delivers the vibrant flavors and laid-back atmosphere of Hawaii in a fast casual setting.
