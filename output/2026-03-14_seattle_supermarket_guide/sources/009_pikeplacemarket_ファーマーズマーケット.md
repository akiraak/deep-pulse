# [*2026 Season Announced!*]{style="color: #a6192e;"} {#season-announced style="text-align: center;"}

Pike Place Market Express Farmers Markets bring the season's best directly to downtown Seattle! Kicking off in June, these small but vibrant markets offer a rotating selection of farm-fresh vegetables, juicy fruits, colorful flowers, local honey, jams, and organic meats, all sourced from the region's finest farmers.

Plus, our popular Produce on Pike market is growing! Every Wednesday evening in July, August, and September, farmers will set up along the north end of Pike Place, offering an unbeatable selection of peak-season produce.

Don't miss your chance to shop fresh and local.
