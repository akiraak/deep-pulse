BELLEVUE, Wash. --- Thursday marks the launch of the largest Asian supermarket in the state of Washington, as T&T Supermarket opens to the public in Bellevue.

T&T, which has over 35 locations in Canada, is opening its flagship U.S. location in the Factoria neighborhood of Bellevue.

The company says the 76,000-square-foot location will offer \"an unrivaled assortment of authentic Asian products, fresh produce, live seafood, an in-store Kitchen and Bakery, and Asian beauty products.\"

Not only is the location the largest Asian supermarket in Washington, it is also the largest T&T location in the world.

According to census data, nearly 40% of Bellevue\'s population is Asian.

\"The Factoria the marketplace at Factoria, Bellevue is a really growing city. It\'s got a huge range of cultural backgrounds. It\'s got great education, great schools, great real estate, very good accessible transportation across different parts of the city. And most importantly, I wanted to come in big and 76,000 (square feet) is dang big,\" CEO Tina Lee said during a media event ahead of Thursday\'s opening.

The store will open its doors at 9 a.m., although some customers may be lining up early to get the first chance to shop.

As part of celebrating the grand opening, T&T will have free \$10 gift cards for the first 500 customers of the Bellevue location. The company also offers a rewards program through its T&T USA app, which can be downloaded through the Apple App Store or Android. By signing up, members get points with every \$20 in-store and online purchase, as well as score exclusive discounts.

While T&T\'s Canadian locations cannot sell alcohol due to the country\'s laws, the Bellevue location will be the first T&T to offer Asian beers, wine and spirits. Items like Taiwanese fruit beer, Japanese whisky and sake along with Korean soju can all be found at the location.

This will be the 37th T&T Supermarket location, but it won\'t be the only western Washington location for too long. T&T has announced it plans to open a second location in Lynnwood in summer 2025.

The West Coast expansion will continue for T&T as it also has announced plans to open a new store in San Jose, California.
