The countdown is over. [T&T Supermarket](https://www.tntsupermarket.us/eng/){target="_blank"}, the largest Asian grocery store chain in Canada, today unveiled its first U.S. location to the public in The Marketplace at Factoria in Bellevue.

On Dec. 4, the supermarket hosted a media preview where employees donning green team-wear were busy preparing the store for its opening.
