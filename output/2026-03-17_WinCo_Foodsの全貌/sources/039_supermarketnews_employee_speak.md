[For employees of ]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[[WinCo Foods](/){.ContentText-BodyTextChunk .ContentText-BodyTextChunk_link discover="true" target="_self"}]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[, Christmas is just around the corner, in July.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[That's when they will receive their annual statements telling them how much money is in the personal retirement accounts being kept in reserve for them by the company.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[WinCo is owned by its workers in an employee stock-ownership program, or ESOP. The Boise, Idaho-based company operates 89 discount stores, with an estimated volume of \$5.3 billion.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Each year the company puts approximately 20% of an employee's total compensation into an ESOP account. Of the 11,000 front-line employees currently in the ESOP, more than 400 have accounts worth more than \$1 million for when they retire, ]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[[Steven L. Goddard]{.ContentText-BodyTextChunk .ContentText-BodyTextChunk_bold}]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[, president and chief executive officer, said.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

["Those who have been in the ESOP for awhile look forward to that annual statement in July," he told ]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[[SN]{.ContentText-BodyTextChunk .ContentText-BodyTextChunk_italic}]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[, "and newer employees see their excitement and anticipation, and that heightens their interest in the ESOP.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

["That interest may not occur immediately, but over the years it becomes very real. Because when people become aware there's \$1 million or \$2 million in someone's account, that certainly creates quite a buzz among the employees."]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[WinCo asked employees to write essays last year on what it meant to them to be part of an ESOP, and several of the 25 essays published in WinCo's employee magazine mentioned the annual statements.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

["He told me, 'WinCo has a bank account with your name on it. They put money in, and all you have to do is keep working, and someday you might be a millionaire.' "]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[[Kirby Myers]{.ContentText-BodyTextChunk .ContentText-BodyTextChunk_bold}]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[, a cashier at the same store, said he was encouraged when he saw "that first profit-sharing statement revealing how well my company did last year as a direct result of the efforts we, the owners, invested. It was encouraging too to see, in actual shares accrued, that my own efforts were rewarded."]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[WinCo has been an ESOP company since 1984, when employees purchased the majority of its shares for \$10.6 million.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Citing data from the National Center for Employee Ownership, the company said ESOP account balances tend to be three to five times higher than the U.S. average for a 401(k) plan.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[It also said employee-owners are more invested in their companies' success, less likely to quit or be laid off and more prepared economically for retirement than those in other industries.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[For the fiscal year ended in March 2012 the ESOP had 64.9 million shares, with a value of \$2.6 billion and an annual growth rate of 21.5% a year.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[According to the company's newsletter, a \$1 investment in Kroger 10 years ago would be worth \$1.75 today, a 6% annual growth rate; \$1 invested 10 years ago in Walmart would be worth two cents more, \$1.77; while \$1 invested in WinCo a decade ago would be worth \$7.30, for an average growth rate of 22%.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Full vesting take five years, with one vested year equivalent to at least 1,000 hours worked during the plan year.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[[Feature Story: WinCo Goes for the Win]{.ContentText-BodyTextChunk .ContentText-BodyTextChunk_bold}]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Goddard said the people WinCo hires are probably not significantly different than those hired by other companies in the industry, "but over time, the ESOP helps them become different, especially once they understand what it means to be part of an employee-owned company."]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Some employees really grasp the concept, and their work reflects their pride of ownership, Goddard said, "while others don't embrace it --- maybe because they took the job to work their way through school or simply because they don't get it.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

["But the vast majority of employees do embrace the concept of ownership, and they will sometimes try to emphasize the concept to those who don't get it --- either by talking to a manager or talking directly to employees who aren't applying themselves on the job the way they should."]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Several WinCo employees wrote in their essays about the pride they take in their work as owners.]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

["I get back what I put in," ]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[[Amy Pearson]{.ContentText-BodyTextChunk .ContentText-BodyTextChunk_bold}]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}[, a deli manager-in-training at a WinCo in Gresham, Ore., said. "I am only one employee out of thousands, but I can make a difference."]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}

[Being an owner, she wrote, "makes me want to try harder to make my department look full and fresh --- to make a point of saying hello to a customer and to make my workplace a good environment for my fellow workers."]{.ContentText .ContentText_variant_bodyNormal testid="content-text"}
