World Foundation Models

## Open Models for World Generation and Understanding {#open-models-for-world-generation-and-understanding .title}

### Cosmos Predict {#cosmos-predict .title}

Leading world generation model, adaptable to any physical AI task or environment.

Generate 30s predictive video worlds from text, image, or video with 2B/14B models, or post-train on your data to create custom edge cases, closed-loop policies, and multiview, robot-centric simulations.

[[Get Started on GitHub]{.btn-text}](https://github.com/nvidia-cosmos/cosmos-predict2.5){#nv-button-a752d209a0 .btn-content .btncta cmp-clickable="false " rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"}

### Cosmos Transfer {#cosmos-transfer .title}

Multicontrol model for simulation to photoreal transformation.

Pair with physical AI simulation frameworks, such as CARLA or [NVIDIA Isaac Sim™](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac/sim){target="_blank"}, to accelerate [synthetic data generation](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/glossary/synthetic-data-generation/) across various environments and lighting conditions.

[[Get Started on GitHub]{.btn-text}](https://github.com/nvidia-cosmos/cosmos-transfer2.5){#nv-button-fa218853e5 .btn-content .btncta cmp-clickable="false " rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"}

### Cosmos Reason {#cosmos-reason .title}

Leading vision language model (VLM) enabling robots and vision AI agents to reason like humans.

Combines prior knowledge, physics, and common sense for real-time alerts and actionable insights across public safety, traffic monitoring, logistics, quality inspection, and physical AI.

[[Get Started on GitHub]{.btn-text}](https://github.com/nvidia-cosmos/cosmos-reason2){#nv-button-c4656d5ac2 .btn-content .btncta cmp-clickable="false " rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"}

### Robot Learning {#robot-learning .title}

Build custom world models for downstream tasks, environments, camera or sensor layouts, and policies.

-   Post-train Cosmos Predict for robot-specific views or control policies
-   Generate synthetic data across environments and lighting conditions with Cosmos Transfer
-   Post-train Cosmos Reason using the [Cosmos RL](https://github.com/nvidia-cosmos/cosmos-rl){target="_blank"} framework to build vision-language-action (VLA) models
-   Create an end-to-end synthetic data augmentation and evaluation pipeline using the [Physical AI Data Factory Blueprint](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-announces-open-physical-ai-data-factory-blueprint-to-accelerate-robotics-vision-ai-agents-and-autonomous-vehicle-development){target="_blank"} built on Cosmos

### Autonomous Vehicle Training {#autonomous-vehicle-training .title}

-   Expand into multi-sensor views using Cosmos Predict
-   Create an end-to-end synthetic data augmentation and evaluation pipeline using the [Physical AI Data Factory Blueprint](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/nvidia-announces-open-physical-ai-data-factory-blueprint-to-accelerate-robotics-vision-ai-agents-and-autonomous-vehicle-development){target="_blank"} built on Cosmos

### Video Analytics AI Agents {#video-analytics-ai-agents .title}

Enhance automation, safety, and operational efficiency across industrial and urban environments.

-   Deliver real-time question-answering and alerts
-   Provide rich contextual insights
-   Extract insights from large-scale video data with NVIDIA Blueprint for [video search and summarization](https://build.nvidia.com/nvidia/video-search-and-summarization){target="_blank"}

### [Can I build a world model from scratch using tools from the Cosmos platform and my custom or in-house foundation model?]{.cmp-accordion__title} []{.cmp-accordion__icon .fa-light .fa-sharp .fa-angle-down} {#can-i-build-a-world-model-from-scratch-using-tools-from-the-cosmos-platform-and-my-custom-or-in-house-foundation-model .cmp-accordion__header}

Yes, you can leverage Cosmos to build from scratch with your preferred foundation model or model architecture. You can start by using [Cosmos Curator](https://github.com/nvidia-cosmos/cosmos-curate){target="_blank"} for video data preprocessing. Then compress and decode your data with [Cosmos tokenizer](https://developer.nvidia.com/blog/state-of-the-art-multimodal-generative-ai-model-development-with-nvidia-nemo/){target="_blank"}. Once you have processed the data, you can train or fine-tune your model. 

Using [NVIDIA NIM™ microservices](https://build.nvidia.com/search?q=%22cosmos%22+-nemotron){target="_blank"}, you can easily integrate your physical AI models into your applications across cloud, data centers, and workstations.

You can also use [NVIDIA DGX Cloud](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/data-center/dgx-cloud/?ncid=pa-srch-goog-542457-DGX-Brand-prsp&_bt=719595959246&_bk=nvidia%20dgx%20cloud&_bm=b&_bn=g&_bg=169380731029&gad_source=1&gclid=EAIaIQobChMI5ofZgLyvigMVlNXCBB0Vsgu8EAAYASAAEgKPbvD_BwE) to train AI models and deploy them anywhere at scale.

### [What are the differences between Cosmos Predict, Cosmos Transfer, and Cosmos Reason, and how do they work together?]{.cmp-accordion__title} []{.cmp-accordion__icon .fa-light .fa-sharp .fa-angle-down} {#what-are-the-differences-between-cosmos-predict-cosmos-transfer-and-cosmos-reason-and-how-do-they-work-together .cmp-accordion__header}

All three are WFMs with distinct roles:

-   Cosmos Predict generates diverse video scenes from text, image, or video prompts---ideal for post-training on subjects like robots or self-driving cars.
-   Cosmos Transfer applies multi-control style transfer---changing lighting and environments---on physics-based videos, often created in simulators like NVIDIA Omniverse™.
-   Cosmos Reason answers queries by reasoning over video and image inputs. Cosmos Reason can generate new and diverse text prompts from one starting video for Cosmos Predict, or critique and annotate synthetic data from Predict and Transfer.

### [What is the difference between Cosmos and Omniverse? ]{.cmp-accordion__title} []{.cmp-accordion__icon .fa-light .fa-sharp .fa-angle-down} {#what-is-the-difference-between-cosmos-and-omniverse .cmp-accordion__header}

Omniverse creates realistic 3D simulations of real-world tasks by using different generative APIs, SDKs, and NVIDIA RTX rendering technology.

Developers can input Omniverse simulations as instruction videos to Cosmos Transfer models to generate controllable photoreal synthetic data.

Together, Omniverse provides the simulation environment before and after training, while Cosmos provides the foundation models to generate video data and train physical AI models.

Learn more about [NVIDIA Omniverse](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/omniverse/).
